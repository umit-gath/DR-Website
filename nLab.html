Course Syllabus

The objective of this lab-based course is to gain theoretical and practical knowledge of a routers architecture, components, and operation, and explains the principles of switching and routing protocols. Students in lab will analyze, configure, verify, and troubleshoot the primary routing protocols Static, RIPv1, RIPv2, EIGRP, and OSPF. Recognize and correct common routing issues and problems .
