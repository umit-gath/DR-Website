Course Syllabus

This course introduces the principles of voice transmission technologies. Topics discussed include: Public switched telephone network, the telephone and the local loop architecture, inter-exchange networks, and signaling. Technology for offering telephony over an internet including: voice-over-IP (VOIP) end points and protocols, end-to-end delay, telephony signaling protocols, gateways and network components, telephone service provision, multi-point, network issues, and the future. Presents market, policy, and economic issues; differentiates VOIP on public or private networks.
